Overview

Sonic the Hedgehog (2020) follows Sonic, a blue hedgehog from another world with super speed, who is forced to hide out on Earth to avoid being caught. When he inadvertently causes a blackout, he is the target of Dr. Robotnik, a scientist determined to harness his powers. Allying with local sheriff Tom Wachowski, Sonic embarks on an escape from Robotnik's clutches, the fate of the world hanging in the balance. Sonic and Tom form a tight bond while evading capture by Robotnik and foiling his evil schemes.

Main Characters

Sonic the Hedgehog

A blue hedgehog who can run at supersonic speeds, is on the run from Dr. Robotnik and the government seeking companionship and a sense of belonging along his journey.

Dr. Ivo Gerald Robotnik

A brilliant, but evil and insane mad scientist who is out to steal Sonic's power and conquer the world, becoming Sonic's arch enemy.

Tom Wachowski

A kind hearted sheriff of green hills, montana and newly appointed officer of san Francisco police department who befriends Sonic to help stop Dr. Robotnik.

Maddie Wachowski

Thomas Wachowski's supportive wife, who also befriends Sonic and helps him and her husband stop Dr. Robotnik.
